Computer Fundamentals Practical Test (20 Marks)

Instructions:

This test involves performing tasks on a computer. Complete all tasks.

Tasks:

File and Folder Management (6 Marks):

  • Create a new folder on the desktop named "ComputerFundamentals".
  • Inside the folder, create three subfolders: "Documents", "Images", and "Downloads".
  • Download a sample image and a sample text document from the internet and save them into the appropriate subfolders.

System Settings (7 Marks):

  • Change the desktop background to a different image.
  • Adjust the screen resolution.
  • Demonstrate how to check the current amount of RAM installed on the computer.
  • Demonstrate how to view the currently running programs.

Internet and Security (7 Marks):

  • Open a web browser and search for a specific topic.
  • Bookmark a useful website.
  • Demonstrate how to check for updates to the operating system.
  • Explain the importance of using strong passwords.

Email Practical Test (10 Marks)

Scenario:

You are an administrative assistant at "Tech Solutions Inc." You need to send an email to a potential client, "Global Innovations," to schedule a meeting.

Tasks:

Compose a New Email (2 Marks):

  • Open your email client and compose a new email.
  • Address the email to: contact@globalinnovations.com
  • Set the subject line to: "Meeting Request: Tech Solutions Inc. - [Your Name]"

Body of the Email (6 Marks):

  • Write a professional and concise email body. Include the following:
    • A brief introduction of yourself and Tech Solutions Inc.
    • A clear statement of the purpose of the email: to schedule a meeting to discuss potential collaboration.
    • Suggest two specific dates and times for the meeting.
    • Request confirmation of their availability.
    • Include your phone number so they can contact you.
    • End with a professional closing (e.g., "Sincerely," or "Best Regards,").

Attachment (2 Marks):

  • Attach a sample document (e.g., a simple text file or a sample company brochure) to the email.
  • Name the attached file "TechSolutions_Info.txt" or similar.
