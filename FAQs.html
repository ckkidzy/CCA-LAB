Frequently Asked Questions

Q.What are the prerequisites for this course?
There are no specific prerequisites for this course. It's designed for beginners with little to no prior experience with computers.
Q.What software will I be learning?
You will gain proficiency in essential software applications like Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Internet browsers.
Q.What is the duration of the course?
The course duration depends on your choice. We provide 1month, 2months and 3months course duration.
Q.Are there any job opportunities after completing this course?
Yes, after completing the course, you'll be equipped with skills suitable for various entry-level positions like Data Entry Operator, Office Assistant, and Customer Support Representative and lots more.
Q.Will I receive a certificate upon completion?
Yes, you will receive a certificate of completion after successfully finishing the course.
Q.Is there any hands-on practice included in the course?
Absolutely! The course includes numerous practical exercises and hands-on projects to reinforce your learning.
Q.What kind of support is provided during the course?
You will have daily 3 hours of classes everday. We help you with any assistance you may need.
Q.Are the course materials available online?
Yes, all course materials, including videos, documents, and exercises, are available online.
Q.Can I access the course materials after the course ends?
Yes, you will have access to the course materials after the course completion too.
Q.What is the class schedule?
Classes are held from Monday to Saturday. 9am-12pm Morning Batch and 1pm-4pm Afternoon Batch.
